Moonlight


I.

Softness upon your silent face

Pale moonlight upon your brow


Have I found the heart you lost?

Is it a darker red than you expected?


It's quiver, an intimate moment

Laid naked upon my hand


I feel every inch of you

Cast across the stars



II.

How deep does it all go?

Is your sadness the same shade as mine?

Does it reflect the sky as it darkens

Around the corners of the horizen?


I know it starts slow

Like a quiet ache, in a place you can't reach

It moves form your toes, to you knees

From your knees, to your heart


It reaches for your throat


There, it sits in a silent corner

The one where a fire once smoldered

Now only ash and embers remain


It's a misty morning you didn't expect


III.

A closed door

A heartbeat

A feeling of content


Please think of me

The way I think of you


By Ezra V.
