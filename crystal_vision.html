Crystal Vision


Cold hands against my face

Warmth within my chest

The minutes grow colder

As the ice draws nearer


How far away

Am I from home?


An echo rings out

Cutting vibrance from the air

A vaporwave of nostalgia

Erupts from my mind


A chemical change

Deep within the skin

Beneath nerve and bone

Within the cell


Do I remain

In your memory?


I see the blue expanse

A clear water

But my mind shifts, alters

A vision morphs, breathes


A crystal palace

Filled with crystal thoughts

Frigid and glistening

It's majesty, pale, ominous


It's heart,

Tender as your eyes



By Ezra V.
